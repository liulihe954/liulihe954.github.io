My name is Lihe Liu (刘力赫), I am currently a first-year PhD student at [Department of Animal and Dairy Sciences](https://andysci.wisc.edu/), College of Agricultural and Life Sciences at University of Wisconsin - Madison. I work with [Dr. Francisco Peñagaricano](http://fpenagaricano-lab.org/). My research interest lies in multi-omics data integration.

I was born and raised in [Zibo, Shandong](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zibo), China. I have ever studied & lived in [Wuhan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wuhan), [Beijing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Beijing) and [Gainesville FL](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gainesville,_Florida).
