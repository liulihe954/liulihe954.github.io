Currently, I just started working on a package for hypergeometric-based over-representation test. It is friendly to weighted correlation network analysis, especially when you have multiple non-preserved modules. Package can help to systematically search for significant hits based on Fisher's exact test. Now I am exploring a few databases, GO, KEGG, Interpro, MeSH and Reactome.
I am thinking of appropriately integrating hierarchical/structure information, as well as measuring the pair-wise relationship of modules (hum, maybe...)

Package hosted on [Github](https://github.com/liulihe954/EnrichKit).
