The 5th Animal Sciences Symposium for graduate students and faculty was held at the Courtyard by Marriott at **St. Augustine Beach, on October 3-4, 2019**. 

We had 113 registered participants (including 67 graduate students, 22 faculty, 6 summer interns and 18 postdocs/visiting fellows/graduate interns), 32 oral presentations and 24 poster presentations. 

The symposium allowed faculty and students to spend time together away from campus, share science and foster collaboration in our research efforts.
